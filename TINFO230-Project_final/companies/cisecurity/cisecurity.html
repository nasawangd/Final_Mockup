    CI Security

    CI Security specilizes in threat detection and human response to cyber attacks and was founded in 2011. When the company was started it was known as M.K. Hamilton & Associates, LLC and was based in Bremerton Washington. The company then changed its name to Critical Informatics as focus shifted to developing cybersecurity products and services.
    Now under the name CI (Critical Insight)Security, the company continues to push the edge in security and technology with its advanced managed detection and response or MDR for short which is a combination of Human experts and next generation processing.

    Vision: To help local, tribal, and territorial governments become more secure and resilient against cybersecurity threats

    The companies morals and values include

    • Putting comunication with the customers first to help improve collaboration
    • Taking mesurments and recording data so they can best serve the customer
    • Taking security seriously so customers don't have to worry
    • Using structured and orginized plans and teams to complete diliverables promptly
    • Keeping diversity in all aspects of their business to promote different perspectives which in turn lead to better outcomes

    What CI Security brings to the table

    CI Security uses a highly dedicated team of security analysts to provide real-time threat detection, investigation, and response to client users. This all happens 24/7 as monitoring on your network never stops so that threats can be detected and delt with quickly. CI Security also uses real-time data dashboards so you may be able to keep track of your networks traffic and any incident alerts that may occur.
