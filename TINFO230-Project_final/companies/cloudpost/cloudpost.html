    ORDR

    ORDR was originally named CloudPost and was founded in November 2015. The company is a developer of security and risk management software. Their main focus is keeping internet of things(IoT) devices and systems for hospitals as well as other companies secure using an AI based systems. They were rebranded as ORDR in February 2019.

    Different Actions that ORDR take

    Identify & Classify

    1. Discovers every device in your environment
    2. Tracks risk scores to focus attention on high risk devices
    3. Maintains a real-time database and tracks changes
    4. Integrates with management and workflow tools

    Regulate

    1. Monitors and analyzes all devices communication 24/7
    2. Learns correct behaviors and creates conversation maps
    3. Group systems by type, location, function, application
    4. Anomaly detection prevents and isolates attempted attacks

    Secure

    1. Micro-segmentation per NIST
    2. Acces control policy generation
    3. Full integration with exisitng NAC solutions
    4. Program firewalls, wired/wireless access network

    System Utilization

    1. Compare usage across facilities to for better distribution
    2. Identify offline devices and bring them back into service
    3. Understand the usage patterns and adjust schedules
    4. Make better-informed purchasing decisions

    AI

    Ordr implements all of these services using the first actionable AI based systems control engine. Ordr Systems Control Engine (SCE) is the only purpose-built solution that fully maps every microscopic device detail and its context – the device flow genome – at massive scale, using machine learning to completely and continuously inspect and baseline the behavior of every device.. Ordr detects exposed vulnerabilities and delivers intricate risk scores for priority attention and mitigation. All in real-time, all-the-time, delivered in an elegantly simple UI.
